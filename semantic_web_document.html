My Favorite Books or Book Series:

Title Genre Author Explanation
The Fate and Flame Series Fantasy K.A. Tucker
  • Unique take on common human entering Fae world trope
  • FMC was not completely clueless
  • Unique take on vampires
  • Great world building and unique magic system
The Sun Eater Series Christopher Ruocchio Science-Fiction (debatable)
  • Phenomenal World Building
  • Marketed as science fiction, epic fantasy in my opinion with a sci-fi setting
  • Unique story structure after book 1
Elantris Brandon Sanderson Fantasy
  • My introduction to Brandon Sanderson
  • Incredibly vivid world building
  • Unique storyline - there's a common theme with my favorite books
The Ever Seas Series L.J. Andrews Fantasy/Romance
  • Pirates, fae, cool magic system, warring families what more could you want from a romantasy
  • Can be read as a duology as the second two books follow a different couple, not incredibly time consuming
The War of Lost Hearts Series Carissa Broadbent Fantasy
  • Yet again, unique world building and magic system
  • Surprising twists throughout series
  • Trilogy, did not take long to get through
  • Completed series
The Lightlark Series Alex Aster Fantasy
  • THE ENDING OF BOOK 2 - I MEAN MY GOD
  • Fun games and trials story with a sprinkle of romance
  • Twists and turns I did not expect throughout the series
  • Book 1 was good, debut book for Aster; book 2 was phenomenal
The Mistborn Saga Brandon Sanderson Fantasy If you know this series, an explanation is not necessary
Books That are Overhyped on TikTok and Instagram:
  1. A Court of Thorns and Roses Series
    • Female main character was obnoxious
    • One of the sisters was utterly useless throughout
    • While I loved the plot and there were notable parts of the book that were great, overall it was MAYBE a 4/5 star series
    • The cult following always says, "it gets better after book 1" which it does but not so much that I will ever understand people making this series their entire personality
    • Rating: 3.5/5
  2. When the Moon Hatched
    • The entire book reads as if the author pulled random words out of a thesaurus and pretended they did not make convaluted sentences
    • Nothing in the book is resolved at the end; one of the longest books I have read in a long time only for NOTHING IMPORTANT TO BE EXPLAINED
    • FMC was annoying
    • Rating: 2.5/5
  3. The Housemaid
    • The book was not bad, just not.. great
    • Kind of predictable
    • Felt like certain parts of the story were thrown in for filler and did not matter for the plot
    • Rating: 3.5/5
  4. Fourth Wing
    • BOOK 2 WAS WAY BETTER
    • FMC was bland
    • Story was a bit drawn out, in my opinion
    • Rating: 3/5
